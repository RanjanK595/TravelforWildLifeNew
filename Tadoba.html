About Tadoba:

Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve is the most famous Tiger Reserve and National Park of Central India.

Located in Chandrapur Dist. It is the oldest and the largest National Park of Maharashtra State.

Very famous for the tiger couple Maya and Matkasur, it has high density of Tiger population across the

core and buffer region. The park has got several entry gates with three different forest range.

Animals that can be sighted are, samber deer, nilgai, spotted deer, leopard, wild dog, marsh crocodile,

 sloth bear, langur, peacock, mongoose, wild boar and many different species of birds.
