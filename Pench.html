Important Pointers:

 

·          Pench has night safari however it be available on additional cost.

·          Pench has 2 range, Madhya Pradesh And Maharashtra with different Core and Buffer zones.

·          There is only one core area in Pench M P, entry gates are Turiya, Karmazari & Jamtara.

·          Maharashtra range has 3 core area, distance of all zone from Turia gate is mentioned below.

·          Khursapar – 20 km / Sillari Core – 40km / Chorbouli Core - 50KM

·          All zones have good Tiger number and sighting chances

·          Camera Fees in Maharashtra Pench is INR 450 for DSLR Lens

·          No camera fee in Madhya Pradesh Pench.

·          If at all it changes Guest has to pay at the gate, Camera fees not included.

Pench National Park is the most famous Tiger Reserve and National Park of Central India.

Located in Khawasa Dist. It is the oldest and the largest National Park of Maharashtra State.

Very famous for the tiger couple ‘Collarwali’ and ‘Baras’, it has high density of Tiger population

across the core and buffer region. The park has got several entry gates with three different forest range.

Animals that can be sighted are, samber deer, nilgai, spotted deer, leopard, wild dog,

marsh crocodile, sloth bear, langur, peacock, mongoose, wild boar and many different species of birds.
